Privacy Policy for StepLoad

Effective date: October 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to StepLoad (“the App”). StepLoad is a workout tracking app that allows users to create workout plans and track their training sessions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email Address – used for authentication and account management.
  • Display Name and Profile Picture – used to personalize your profile within the app.
  • Photos – if you choose to upload or take a photo for your profile picture.

3. How We Collect Information

Information is collected when you create an account or sign in using Firebase Authentication, update your profile information, or upload/take a photo using your device’s camera or photo library.

4. Use of Information

We use your information to create and manage your account, display your name and profile picture within the app, and provide customer support. Your data will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate StepLoad:

  • Firebase Authentication and Storage (by Google LLC)

6. App Permissions

  • Camera Access: to allow you to take a profile photo.
  • Photo Library Access: to allow you to choose an existing photo for your profile.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your personal information is retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove your personal data from our systems.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data, including the use of secure connections (HTTPS) and Firebase’s built-in encryption and access controls.

9. Children’s Privacy

StepLoad is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 years old.
